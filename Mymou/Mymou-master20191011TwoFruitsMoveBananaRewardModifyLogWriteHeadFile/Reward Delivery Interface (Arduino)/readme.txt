C# code to control the reward-delivery interface (Arduino Uno board)

Currently configured to control digital out channels 2-5

Listens for a single digit string from bluetooth and executes the corresponding actions:

Bluetooth input		Output
0			All low
1			2 high
2			2 low
3			3 high
4			3 low
5			4 high
6			4 low
7			5 high
8			5 low
